Suhwan Cho

I am a Research Scientist at GenGenAI, Seoul, Korea. Previously, I was a Research Scientist Intern in the Deep Learning Group at Adobe Research, San Jose, California, USA. I earned my Ph.D. from Yonsei University, Seoul, Korea. My research focuses on video editing technologies, including video object segmentation and video inpainting.

I am always open to collaborations! Please feel free to contact me if you are interested :)
